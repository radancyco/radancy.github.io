Quality Assurance and Accessibility

All patterns, layouts, and functionality are tested against our supported browsers. We also look for accessibility issues via automated and manual testing tools. TMP is committed to the accessibility of it's websites and products and strives to meet as much of WCAG 2.0 Level AA as possible. If you discover any problems with our work, please open up an issue and let us know about it or contact Michael Spellacy.
