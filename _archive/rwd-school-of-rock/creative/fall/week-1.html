A Note on Adaptive

This training focuses on responsive web design - fluid design with no one target screen size. Some site concepts are more suited for adaptive design - a site with only one or two major breakpoints.

In general, you should aim for your design to be fully responsive, but sometimes you may need to take an adaptive approach. It's best to discuss which route to take with your friendly neighborhood developer.

  • Adaptive example (note how elements are static but positions change as screen resizes): screamingfrog.co.uk
  • Responsive example (note how all elements resize with browser screen): css-tricks.com
